Bridging the Tech Divide

Karigar.Connect™

How do we connect offline rural artisans with global online buyers? By utilizing tools they already trust: a phone call and a text message.

The moment you purchase, the weaver receives a low-bandwidth SMS containing an OTP and order customizations (colors, size, notes). For weavers who cannot read, they can request an automated voice call reading the details back to them in their local language.
